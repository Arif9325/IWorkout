# **IWorkout - Personal Trainers**

### **Featuring: Marcus, Sayed Arif Shah, and Jayson**

---

### **App Overview**
**IWorkout** is a user-friendly workout tracking app designed to help you easily create, manage, and view your personalized workout routines. Stay organized and motivated by planning your fitness journey one day at a time!

---

## **How It Works**

### **Login**
Start by logging in to your account. This ensures a personalized experience where your workouts are securely stored.  
<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1f4dfdcc-5129-4329-9025-6110959a5ebe" alt="Login Screen" width="500"/>

---

### **Sign Up**
New here? No problem! Create an account in the **Sign-Up** section. Once registered, you'll be redirected to the login page, where you can sign in to get started.  
<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/23c7436d-aac1-4f9c-95f9-32d9db65d433" alt="Sign-Up Screen" width="500"/>

---

### **Home Page**
Once logged in, you'll land on the **Home Page**, which greets you with a personalized message (e.g., "Hello, [User]!").  
From here, you can:
- **Log out** of your account.  
- **Chat with the AI** for fitness tips and guidance.  
- **Add a workout** to your weekly plan.  
- **View your workout routines** for the week.  

<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7970e951-16fe-4b1d-8b63-a786698ec4ef" alt="Home Page" width="500"/>

---

### **Workout Planner**
Plan your week by adding workouts to specific days (Monday to Sunday).  
Features include:
- Select a day of the week.  
- Add multiple workouts for a single day.  

This flexibility helps you build a detailed and effective workout routine.  
<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/32e65ffb-5071-4e3b-8af6-cc3d99773157" alt="Workout Planner" width="500"/>

---

### **View Workouts**
The **View Workouts** section lets you:
- Select a specific day to see your planned workouts.  
- Edit or delete workouts as needed.  

This feature gives you complete control over your fitness plans, ensuring they remain up-to-date and aligned with your goals.  
<img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a2b6c7ae-bcca-44f8-80e2-2fee1e9b8e12" alt="View Workouts" width="500"/>
